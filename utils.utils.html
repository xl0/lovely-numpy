pretty_str

 pretty_str (x)

A slightly better way to print float-y values. Works for np.ndarray, torch.Tensor, jax.DeviceArray, and scalars.

nasties = randoms[:12].copy()

nasties[0] *= 10000
nasties[1] /= 10000
nasties[3] = float('inf')
nasties[4] = float('-inf')
nasties[5] = float('nan')
nasties = nasties.reshape((2,6))
pretty_str(nasties)
'[[1.764e+04, 4.002e-05, 0.979, inf, -inf, nan], [0.950, -0.151, -0.103, 0.411, 0.144, 1.454]]'
test_eq(pretty_str(nasties), '[[1.764e+04, 4.002e-05, 0.979, inf, -inf, nan], [0.950, -0.151, -0.103, 0.411, 0.144, 1.454]]')
